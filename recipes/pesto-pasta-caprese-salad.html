Pesto Pasta Caprese Salad

A quick, light, and delicioius salad, perfect for summer entertaining.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups rotini pasta
  • 3 tablespoons pesto
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup small fresh mozzarella balls
  • 2 leaves fresh basil leaves, shredded

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook rotini at a boil until tender yet firm, about 8 minutes, and then drain.
  3. Mix pesto, olive oil, salt, garlic, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Toss rotini with mixture, and then fold in tomatoes, mozzarella, and fresh basil.
