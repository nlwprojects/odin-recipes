Sweet Spinach Muffins

Description

A delicious way to incorporate more greens into your day! This quick muffin recipe is so good, even my kids like them.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 3/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 6 oz spinach
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp cinanamon
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    • Steps

    1. Preheat ove to 350 F and grease your muffin pan.
    2. Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl and mix.
    3. Add milk, melted oil, banana, honey, egg, and vanilla to a blender and blend until completely smoooth.
    4. Pour the blended ingredients into the dry ingredients and fold in gently to combine.
    5. Pour batter into the greased muffin pan and bake 18-20 minutes or until the muffins bounce back when you touch the top.
    6. Remove from the oven and let sit 10 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
