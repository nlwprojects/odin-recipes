Baked French Toast

A little twist on a breakfast favorite. Pop your french toast in the oven instead of standing at the stove while cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut diagonally in 1 inch slices
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 2 tbs butter

Cinnamon Sugar Topping

  • 1 tbsp granualted sugar
  • 1/2 cinnamon

Steps

  1. Grease a 9x13" baking dish and arrange slices of bread on the bottom.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, cream, maple syrup, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt until combined.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture and place it into the dish, overlapping and angled slightly
  4. After all the bread slices are aranged in the dish, put the remaining mixture over top.
  5. Cover with aluminum foil and refridgerate for 30 minutes, up to overnight.
  6. Preheat over to 350 F
  7. Bake the French toast, still covered, for 30 minutes.
  8. While the French toast is in the over, melt the 2 tbsp of butter, and make the cinnamon sugar topping by combining 1 tbsp sugar and 1/2 tsp cinammon.
  9. Remove French toast from oven, pour the melted butter over top and sprinkle with the cinnamon sugar mixture.
  10. Return to oven, uncovered, and bake for 10 minutes until the tips are golden and the bread looks slightly puffed up.
  11. Cool for 5 minutes and serve with your favorite toppings.
